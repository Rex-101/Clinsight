The Extraction Pipeline

How ClinSight transforms raw text into structured clinical intelligence

1

Preprocessing Raw Text

Clean and standardize input by removing noise, normalizing medical abbreviations, and preparing text for analysis. This ensures consistent, high-quality data for downstream processing.

2

Named Entity Recognition (NER)

Automatically detect and extract key clinical entities including symptoms, diagnoses, medications, dosages, and procedures using advanced NLP models trained on medical literature.

3

Sentiment & Trend Analysis

Track patient condition over time by analyzing sentiment and temporal patterns. Identify whether symptoms are improving, worsening, or remaining stable to support clinical decision-making.

4

Semantic Interoperability

Map extracted entities to standardized medical terminologies including ICD-10, SNOMED CT, and UMLS. This ensures compatibility with existing healthcare systems and enables data exchange.

5

Final Output Generation

Generate comprehensive, structured patient summaries with key clinical findings, risk factors, and recommended next steps. Output is ready for integration into EHR systems or clinical workflows.
